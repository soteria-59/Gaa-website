Anthony DePass Ph.D

Dr. Anthony DePass is the Director of Understanding Interventions that Broaden Participation in Science (UI). The annual UI conferences serve as a major venue for the dissemination of scholarship related to STEM Diversity interventions research, as well as facilitating training activities for evaluators, interventions researchers, and program administrators. He is CEO and Co-Founder of Lifestyle Learning™, an Educational Technology company that develops platforms for career exploration (Career Navideer, Transition Navideer) and student success (Student Success Modules). Recently retired as Professor of Biology at Long Island University in New York, he currently consults with several institutions through DePass Academic Consulting where he leads a group that provides service and training in areas of professional development, grantsmanship, grantsmanship training, and program evaluation.

Over the last three decades, Dr. DePass has led several externally funded training and development programs including a multi-million-dollar national effort that provided training and career development activities spanning the undergraduate to the professional levels for participants from over 140 institutions. His passion for seeking solutions for access, equity, and excellence in STEM education is firmly grounded by his experiences and scholarship in this area.

Welcome to our consulting firm, where we bring together experienced professionals with expertise across various domains in the public and private sectors to help you develop, transform, and thrive.

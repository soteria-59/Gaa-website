Ambassador Ralph S. Thomas

Ambassador Thomas served as Jamaica’s Ambassador to the United State’s of America and Permanent Representative to the Organization of American States until 2016 and was former Ambassador to China and High Commissioner to Singapore He was Ambassador Designate to Thailand, Vietnam, Laos, Cambodia, North Korea, Bangladesh and Pakistan. Ambassador Thomas had an extensive career as a Senior Executive on Wall Street with the Bank of New York Mellon and established a proven track record in business strategy, banking and finance and investments. He advised Multinational Corporations and mid-sized businesses on international business and investments, structured financing of international transactions and projects and headed a Retail Banking franchise.

He served the Mona School of Business as the school’s Director of Strategy and Senior Teaching Fellow for many over a decade focusing on the academic areas of Banking and Finance, Business Strategy, Business in A Global Environment and Enterprise Risk Management at both Graduate and Undergraduate levels. He also provided consulting services in curriculum design and development for institutions in the USA and the Caribbean. As a member of the MSBM Consulting Team, Ambassador Thomas provided business strategy consulting to financial institutions and companies in other industries in the Caribbean, resulting in improved profitability and financial and operational resilience.

He has a Masters in Business Administration (MBA) from Columbia University in the USA and a Bachelor’s Degree from the University of the West Indies. Ambassador Thomas was trained in Financial Analysis and Policy at the IMF Institute of the IMF in Washington as a Central Banker, a unique complement to his Commercial Banking experience and training. He provided consulting Services to the government and the Private Sector based on his expertise.

Ambassador Thomas served on the Board of the Urban Development Corporation, The National Investment Fund of Jamaica, the Jamaica International Financial Services Authority, the Mental Health Association of Westchester, Chairman of the Board of Micro Investment Agency of Jamaica and Member of the Atlanta-Jamaica Chamber of Commerce. Ambassador Thomas is a member of the Academic Review Committee of the Board of Directors of the Jamaica Stock Exchange and a member of the Governing Council of the Corporate Learning Campus of the National Commercial Bank.

A resident of Atlanta, Ambassador Thomas’ expertise is available to consult on value creation and analysis drawing on his multi-dimensional expertise as an Ambassador and extensive international network.
